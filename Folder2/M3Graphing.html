The aspect of interest, portrayed by the light-red span and bordered by two dashed lines, identifies the date range of Dec 2007 to June 2009. This range corresponds with the timeline of the 2007-2009 "Great Recession". During this time, Americans faced the subprime mortgage crisis and the housing bubble burst. The highlighted region in the plot shows an intruiging trend: despite the significant decline of U.S. private construction spending during and after the Great Recession, public construction spending remained constant and, in fact, appears to have increased slightly.
